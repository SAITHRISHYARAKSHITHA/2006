CROP-NEST' journey

Helping farmers deliver their products to customers for 10 years.

1. Awareness Stage

  • Marketing Campaigns: Social media ads, collaboration with agricultural fairs.
  • Word of Mouth: Encourage satisfied customers to share experiences.
  • Community Engagement: Workshops on sustainable practices.

2. Consideration Stage

  • Website Access: Learn about delivery options and benefits.
  • Free Trials: Offering limited-time free delivery trials.
  • Testimonials: Success stories of existing farmer-partners.

3. Onboarding Stage

  • Easy Registration: Simple online or in-person sign-up.
  • Training Sessions: Training on using the platform.
  • Customer Support: Assistance for initial setup.

4. Delivery Stage

  • Order Management: Farmers receive and manage orders.
  • Scheduling Deliveries: Flexibility in delivery scheduling.
  • Logistics: Transportation solutions or partnerships.

5. Customer Experience Stage

  • Feedback Collection: Regular surveys for feedback.
  • Quality Assurance: Implement quality checks.
  • Loyalty Programs: Rewards for repeat purchases.

6. Growth Stage

  • Partnerships: Collaborate with local restaurants and stores.
  • Product Diversification: Encourage new products and value-added offerings.
  • Marketing Support: Assist farmers with marketing.

7. Sustainability and Community Impact Stage

  • Sustainability Initiatives: Promote organic practices.
  • Community Programs: Support local food initiatives.
  • Annual Celebrations: Celebrate farmers and showcase products.

8. Continuous Support Stage

  • Regular Check-Ins: Periodic follow-ups for satisfaction.
  • Resource Sharing: Provide educational resources.
  • Networking Opportunities: Create platforms for farmers to connect.
